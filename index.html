Instructions

  • Use up arrow for tilt up, down arrow for tilt down, left arrow for pan left and right arrow for pan right.
  • Use w and s to change the height of camera, a stands for moving left and d stands for moving right. Also, pressing
    i will move forward the camera and pressing o will move the camera backwards.
  • User could use the control pane in the upper part to switch on/off the headlight, adjust the parameter for user-adjusted
    light source and change the lighting and shading method.
